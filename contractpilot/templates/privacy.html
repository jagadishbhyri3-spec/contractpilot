Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

ContractPilot Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. We comply with the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

ContractPilot Ltd is the data controller for personal data processed through the Service. Our registered address is available upon request. For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@contractpilot.co.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

When you register, we collect: name, email address, and password (hashed). When you subscribe, we collect billing information processed securely by Stripe — we do not store full card details.

3.2 Contract Data

You may upload contract documents (PDFs, text files) for analysis. This data is processed to provide the Service and is stored securely. You retain all ownership rights to your contracts.

3.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage data including: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and feature usage. This helps us improve the Service.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To process transactions and send billing notifications
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on: (a) Contract — to fulfill our terms of service; (b) Consent — for marketing communications (which you can withdraw anytime); (c) Legitimate Interests — for security, fraud prevention, and service improvement; (d) Legal Obligation — where required by law.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Google Cloud / AI Studio — for AI contract analysis (contract text is transmitted securely; see our Security page)
  • Render — our cloud hosting provider
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or court order

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Contract data is retained until you delete it or your account. After account deletion, we remove personal data within 30 days, except where legal obligations require longer retention. Backups may retain data for up to 90 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: (a) Access your personal data; (b) Rectify inaccurate data; (c) Erasure ("right to be forgotten"); (d) Restrict processing; (e) Data portability; (f) Object to processing; (g) Withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email dpo@contractpilot.co.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including: TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for data at rest, password hashing with bcrypt, regular security audits, and access controls. See our Security page for full details.

10. International Transfers

Your data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt). Some third-party services (e.g., Google AI, Stripe) may process data outside the EU. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.

11. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

12. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, contact us immediately.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Service. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.

14. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@contractpilot.co.
