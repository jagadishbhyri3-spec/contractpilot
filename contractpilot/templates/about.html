About ContractPilot

We believe every founder, sales leader, and operations professional deserves to understand what's in their contracts — without waiting days for legal review.

Our Mission

ContractPilot was inspired by Google Cloud's 1,302 AI Use Cases — a collection of real-world applications where AI is transforming how businesses operate. We saw contract negotiation as one of the highest-impact, most underserved use cases.

Our mission is simple: turn days of legal review into minutes, giving deal-makers the confidence to negotiate faster and sign smarter.

How It Works

1

Upload

Drop your PDF or text contract. We handle NDAs, MSAs, SaaS agreements, vendor contracts, and more.

2

Analyze

Our AI, powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Pro, scans every clause, identifies risks, and scores the contract 0–100.

3

Negotiate

Get redlined suggestions for every risky clause. Copy, edit, and send to your counterparty.

Built for Professionals

ContractPilot is used by founders closing their first enterprise deal, sales teams reviewing vendor agreements, and operations managers handling high-volume contract workflows. Whether you're a solo founder or a 100-person team, our AI scales with you.

Trust & Security

Your contracts are sensitive. That's why we built ContractPilot with enterprise-grade security from day one: EU data hosting, TLS 1.3 encryption, bcrypt password hashing, and GDPR compliance. We never use your contract data to train AI models.

Learn more on our Security page.
