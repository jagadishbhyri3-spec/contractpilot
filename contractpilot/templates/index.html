How accurate is the AI analysis? +

Our AI is powered by Gemini 2.5 Pro and trained on thousands of real-world contracts. It achieves 95% accuracy in risk detection and provides redlines based on industry-standard legal precedents. However, we always recommend having a qualified attorney review critical contracts before signing.

Where is my contract data stored? +

All data is stored exclusively in Frankfurt, Germany, within the European Union. We are fully GDPR compliant, use TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit, and follow SOC 2 aligned security controls. Your contracts never leave the EU, and we never use your data to train our models.

How are redlines generated? +

Redlines are generated by comparing your clause against millions of analyzed contracts and legal precedents. The AI identifies deviations from market-standard terms and suggests language that better protects your position — similar to what a senior attorney would recommend.

What types of contracts can I upload? +

We support NDAs, MSAs, SaaS agreements, employment contracts, vendor agreements, licensing deals, and more. You can upload PDFs, Word documents, or paste plain text. Our AI handles both structured and unstructured contract formats.

Is there really no credit card required? +

Absolutely. Your first contract analysis is completely free with no credit card required. We want you to experience the value before making any commitment. After your free analysis, you can choose a plan that fits your volume.
